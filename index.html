What is Worse than Dark Souls?

Worse than Dark Souls is a game that is harder than the very popular game series Dark Souls although the game Worse than Dark Souls is nothing like Dark Souls. The only reason that it is called Worse than Dark Souls is because it is much harder than Dark Souls depending on the difficulty that you are playing on. Instead of being a really hard western style rpg, it's a shooter where you have to shoot boxes that can get really difficult.

Currently there are quite a few glitches like boxes will spawn up against the wall where you can't hit the boxes but I will hopefully fix these issues in the near future.
